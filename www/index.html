How does Giffon work?

From Start

Choose a or any of the millions items listed in . Start a Giffon campaign and let your friends know.

To Surprise

At the end of the campaign, if there are enough pledges, Giffon will collect the money and buy you the gift!


P.S.

In case there are not enough pledges, you can still pay for the remaining amount and get it anyway , or just cancel the campaign and no one will be charged.
